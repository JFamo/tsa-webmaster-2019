Boyertown TSA

Boyertown High School's TSA chapter is one of the premier TSA chapters in the nation, competing and achieving at high levels ever since its early inception into the Pennsylvania Technology Student Association.

This site includes an overview of chapter history, information, activities, fundraisers, and awards, as well as details of the STEM courses, teachers, and projects that students can expect here at Boyertown High School.
