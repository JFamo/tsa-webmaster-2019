WHO IS TOMMEGUN

Luke Tomme, known by his stage name TommeGun, is a rapper, hip-hop artist, and producer based out of Boyertown, Pennsylvania. He has released more than fifteen tracks and acculumated a large following. TommeGun has been featured in various song collaborations and is available for business inquiries.
