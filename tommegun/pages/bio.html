ABOUT L.T. Skiddle

Luke Tomme, better known by his stage name L.T. Skiddle, is a rapper, hip-hop artist, and producer based out of Boyertown, Pennsylvania. L.T. Skiddle started producing music in 2015, which was also the year that he released his first song on soundcloud: KnowDaWae. As he continued to release more music, he grew connections within the music production community such as Mythra Music and No Rest Productions, both of whom L.T. Skiddle has created songs in collaboration with.

L.T. Skiddle has released over 15 tracks and has accumulated thousands of followers over multiple different platforms including SoundCloud, YouTube, Instagram, and Twitter. L.T. Skiddle is available for business inquiries of creating and leasing the rights to his music to those who need it for very affordable prices.
