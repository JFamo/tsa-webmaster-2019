Awards


Inspiration Award - States

BASH-TSA won the Pennsylvania State Inspiration Award in 2017 for having the most positive community involvement of any state chapter.

Purple Award

BASH-TSA won the Purple Award the past three years in a row for raising over $2,000 each year for the American Cancer Society to forward cancer research.

Inspiration Award - Regionals

BASH-TSA won the Pennsylvania Region 9 Inspiration Award in 2017 for its fundraising.

Regional Conference

At the regional level, BASH-TSA semi-finalized in 70 events and took top 3’s in 42 events.

State Conference

At the state level, BASH-TSA semi-finalized in 35 events and took top 3’s in 15 events.

National Conference

At the 2018 National Conference, BASH-TSA were finalists in many events including 6th place in Chapter Team, 4th in Scientific Visualization, and 5th in Software Development.
